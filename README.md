# HorizonTechX_Data-Visualization-Dashboard-

# Sales Performance Dashboard using Power BI

## Project Overview

This project was developed as part of the Horizon TechX Artificial Intelligence & Data Science Internship Program.

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive dashboards and visualizations. The dashboard helps analyze sales performance, profitability, product trends, and regional performance to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel / CSV
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Visualization
* Business Intelligence

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains sales transaction records with the following fields:

* Order_ID
* Order_Date
* Product
* Category
* Quantity
* Sales
* Profit
* Region

Total Records: 1000

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard includes the following KPIs:

* Total Sales: 4,015,502
* Total Profit: 710,085
* Total Orders: 1,000
* Profit Margin: 17.68%

---

## Dashboard Visualizations

### 1. Sales by Region

Analyzes sales performance across different regions.

### 2. Profit by Region

Identifies the most profitable regions.

### 3. Sales by Category

Shows category-wise revenue contribution.

### 4. Profit by Category

Highlights the most profitable product categories.

### 5. Top Products by Sales

Displays the highest-performing products.

### 6. Monthly Sales Trend

Tracks sales performance over time.

### 7. Interactive Filters

Users can filter data by:

* Region
* Category
* Product

---

## Business Insights

* Technology category generated the highest sales revenue.
* Technology products contributed the highest profit.
* West region recorded the highest sales performance.
* West region also achieved the highest profitability.
* Monitor and Office Chair emerged as top-performing products.
* The overall profit margin remained healthy at approximately 17.68%.
